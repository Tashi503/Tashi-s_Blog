Poems

Empty Chair


An empty chair I kept
Just infront of me
Hoping you would come
I stand there waiting for you


You stood little far away
I saw you look right and left
Then you saw others empty chair
And my chair left empty forever


I waited in the cold breeze
Just freezing to walk with you
But I ssaw you with her
Which killed me inside


I wanted to protect you
But just as I reach for you
Your angle protected you
And I was not needed for you anymore

Wonder Bird

You are the bird
That is loved by everyone
When you hum
Everyone listens to you

When you landed on me
Everyone started to look at me too
Everyone said “wow”
They look wonderful together

But I didn’t know that
You are the wonder bird
Who had many places
Which are filled with loves

I was just one
A small part of your wonderland
Who thought of forever
But you changed to never
